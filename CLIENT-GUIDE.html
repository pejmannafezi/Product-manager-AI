AI PM Command Center

A Simple Guide — what this app does and how it helps you

No technical knowledge needed

What is this app, in one sentence?

It's a single place to plan, run, and govern your AI projects — especially in sensitive fields like healthcare — so nothing important slips through the cracks. Think of it as a smart project office that also knows the rules and best practices and helps you follow them.

How it helps you (the big picture)

  • Keeps all your project work in one place — tasks, plans, risks, decisions.
  • Has built-in expert knowledge (a 23-chapter guide) you can search anytime.
  • Checks your project against good practice before you start, so you fix gaps early.
  • Can use AI for smarter suggestions — but works perfectly fine without it.
  • Speaks English and Persian, and works on desktop and phone.

What each part does

You move between parts using the menu at the top of the screen.

🧭 Dashboard — your daily home base

A quick "morning coffee" overview: your top 3 priorities, anything overdue, open problems, high risks, decisions waiting on you, and today's checklist — plus a live feed of what the app did for you.

Why it helps: you start each day knowing exactly what matters, without digging.

📋 Projects — the home for each piece of work

Every project lives here. Open one to find tabs for:

  • Tasks — the to-do list for that project.
  • Plan — milestones (big checkpoints), deliverables, recommended team, and risks.
  • Timeline — a visual schedule showing what happens when and which steps are time-critical.
  • Roadmap — what you're doing now, next, and later.
  • Risks, Decisions, Blockers — what could go wrong, choices made, and anything stuck.
  • Compliance, Documents, Activity — your standards score, files, and full history.

Why it helps: everything about a project is in one organized place.

✅ Tasks — your to-do list

See and manage your action items. Tick them off as you go.

Why it helps: clear, simple progress tracking.

☑️ Checklists — daily, weekly, and monthly routines

Ready-made routines for recurring good habits. Anything you don't finish carries over to the next day automatically.

Why it helps: builds discipline and consistency without extra effort.

📚 Knowledge — your built-in expert library

A full 23-chapter guide on managing AI products, fully searchable. Type a question and get the most relevant sections, with matching words highlighted.

Why it helps: expert guidance is always a search away.

📖 References — add your own documents

Upload or paste your own materials (policies, templates, past documents). The app uses them when giving suggestions. You choose whether a document applies to all projects or just one.

Why it helps: the app's advice becomes tailored to your organization's rules.

🔍 Glossary — plain-language definitions

Quick definitions of AI and product terms.

Why it helps: no more getting lost in jargon.

🛡️ Compliance — a readiness check before you start

Upload a project document and the app reviews it against a governance checklist. You get a score and grade, a list of what's missing, and recommendations — each pointing to the exact guide chapter that explains it.

Why it helps: you catch problems before the project starts, when they're cheap to fix.

🔥 Issues — a guided way to solve problems

Log a problem and the app helps you handle it properly: it rates urgency, auto-creates the right tasks for serious ones, and gives you a checklist you must complete before a problem can be closed.

Why it helps: problems get solved thoroughly and on time — not forgotten.

➕ New Project Intake — the "start a project" button

Tell the app about a new project (and optionally upload a document), and it does the heavy lifting at once:

  • Reviews it for compliance and readiness.
  • Suggests relevant reading from the guide.
  • Turns gaps into a prioritized to-do list with due dates.
  • Builds a roadmap, a full dated plan with milestones, and a risk list.
  • Sets up your daily / weekly / monthly checklists.
  • Writes a starting strategy outline.

Why it helps: days of setup happen in moments, and every project starts on solid footing.

Good to know

  • Works without AI. Fully usable on its own. Turn AI on for smarter suggestions (you'll see an "AI-enhanced" badge); if AI is ever unavailable, nothing breaks.
  • Two languages. Switch between English and Persian anytime from the top bar.
  • Light and dark mode. It matches your device automatically.
  • Works on your phone. The menu collapses into a tidy ☰ button on small screens.
  • Privacy reminder. On a shared online demo, don't upload real patient or confidential data.

Getting started in 3 steps

  1. Open New Project Intake and describe your project (upload a document if you have one).
  2. Review what the app generated — compliance check, plan, roadmap, tasks, and reading list.
  3. Use the Dashboard daily to stay on top of priorities, and tick off your Checklists.

That's it — you're running a well-governed AI project.
